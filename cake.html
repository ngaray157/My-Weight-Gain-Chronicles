This food is a weight putter-onner almost at least once a year. You know, since everyone has a birthday and who doesn’t eat cake for their birthday? And best of all, you can get any size pieces of cake whether you want half the cake or a very thin slice, its very easy to form the slice that appeals to you.

Cake has never had effects on me. I have a lot of family members so I get birthday cake 87% of the year. I can eat like two whole slices of cake and still not be full.
