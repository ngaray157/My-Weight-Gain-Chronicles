Okay, you either love candy or you’re inhuman and weren’t raised correctly. Chocolate and fruity/ sour candies are the only kinds around. Name brands include Kitkat, Hershey, Twix, Smarties, Starbursts, and more. Candy is more popular around Halloween season since people hand it out to others throughout the neighborhood. You can go to any store and there will be one whole aisle dedicated to all candies you could possibly think of. If you really want candy in your life, just buy an entire bag of Halloween candy, hand out 10 out of the 85 pieces inside, and then keep the rest in your house somewhere safe. Candy is also very popular with movies. Instead of buying a $10 4 oz. box of chocolate from the movies, you can go to any Walgreens or CVS nearby and just buy them for a $1.25 each and sneak them into the movies. Totally worth the extra time if you’re really frugal and want to save money.
